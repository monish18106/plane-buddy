The World's Best-Selling Piston Aircraft

The Cirrus SR22 is a single-engine, four- or five-seat composite aircraft that has become one of the most successful general aviation planes ever built. Introduced in 2001, it offers an unmatched combination of performance, technology, and safety. With its advanced avionics and the signature Cirrus Airframe Parachute System (CAPS), the SR22 redefined what private aviation could be.

Notable Features

  • Equipped with the Cirrus Airframe Parachute System (CAPS) — a whole-aircraft recovery parachute.
  • Composite construction for lightweight strength and aerodynamic efficiency.
  • Advanced Garmin Perspective+ avionics suite with large glass cockpit displays.
  • Luxurious, car-like cabin with air conditioning, USB ports, and premium finishes.
  • Modern safety innovations, including flight envelope protection and autothrottle (in G6 models).

Legacy and Impact

The Cirrus SR22 revolutionized the light aircraft industry by combining luxury, safety, and innovation. It remains the best-selling general aviation aircraft of the 21st century and continues to evolve with cutting-edge technology and avionics improvements.

Known for its balance between performance and safety, the SR22 is trusted by private pilots, flight schools, and business owners worldwide. It symbolizes modern general aviation — practical, safe, and beautiful to fly.
